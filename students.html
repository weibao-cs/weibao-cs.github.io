Information for potential students

I welcome self-motivated students to contact me about potential opportunities, such as honours projects, capstone projects, and PhD positions. If you are interested, please me your transcript and CV.

Updates in Jan. 2020: We are currently offering PhD stipend with tuition fee waiving. Please send me your transcript and CV if you are interested.
